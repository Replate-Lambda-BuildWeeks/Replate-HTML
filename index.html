WHAT IS REPLATE?

Often times, restaurants and catered events are left with excess food that goes to waste. REPLATE was established as a way to easily donate excess food to those who specialize in food redistribution.

HOW CAN I GET INVOLVED?

AS A BUSINESS

If you are a restaurant or business ready to schedule a food pickup at your convienence, click the sign up button below.

AS A VOLUNTEER or NON-PROFIT

Volunteers and non-profit organizations looking for food available for pickup, click the sign up button below.
